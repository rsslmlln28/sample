What is a Computer?

 

A computer is an electronic machine that can receive and store data, processes that data according to its given instructions, which in turn, generates a result that are sent to an output device.

 

It operates under the instructions of the programs installed in its memory. It can only work based on the instructions issued and cannot work on its own.

 

 

 

Advantages and Disadvantages of Computers

It's no wonder why computers have become a part of our daily lives, since it made life so much easier, however, there are also downside to it. Let us now address the advantages and disadvantages of computers.

 

 

 

 

Advantages of Computers

 

 

 

Highspeed

 

 

Computers calculate on lightning speeds that can even calculate a very large amount of data in seconds. It can perform millions of calculations a second, so much faster than a man that spends months doing the same thing.

 

 

 

Accuracy

 

 

Aside from being extremely fast, computers calculate with utmost accuracy. Its calculations are completely error free, as long as the data input was correct.

 

 

 

Storage Capability

 

 

Computers can store large amounts of dataso much that even a human brain cant even compete. It can store various types of data, such as text, images, videos, audio etc.

 

 

 

Diligence

 

 

Compared to humans, computers are completely insusceptible to monotony, fatigue, and lack of concentration. It can perform the same task over and over again without getting bored and without losing speed and accuracy.

 

 

 

Versatile

 

 

Computers are very flexible machineswell, not physically, but rather it has a wide variety of abilities. Like, you may challenge it for a game of chess, and then watch a video right after.

 

 

 

Reliability

 

 

Since a computer is a machine, unlike living beings, it doesnt rot and therefore it can function longer. Latest computer models are designed so that it would be easy maintain.

 

 

 

Automation

 

 

Computers can execute given tasks automatically, even without human interaction. As long as a program is given to the computer, it can execute the program based on the instructions it contains.

 

 

 

Reduction in Paperwork and Cost

 

 

By using computers for data processing, it can lessen paper work by a great deal. This will lead to faster results and less processing time. It can also save time on maintaining paper files, since data that are stored digitally on a computer can be retrieved when needed. Although installation of a computer is quite expensive, it would substantially reduce each transaction cost.

 

 

 

 

Disadvantages of Computers

 

 

 

No I.Q.

 

 

Computers are machines that have no intelligence. It cannot make a decision or do anything on its own, and it needs to be instructed for it to perform a task.

 

 

 

Dependency

 

 

 

Computers function based on user instruction, which makes it human dependent.

 

 

 

Environment

 

 

 

Computers cannot work on just every environment. It should have a suitable environment for it to function properly without getting damaged.

 

 

 

No Feeling

 

 

 

Computers are machines and therefore cannot feel emotion. Unlike humans, it cannot make a judgement based on feeling, taste, experience or knowledge.
